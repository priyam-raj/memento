Overview

Priyam Raj is a serial founder and operator of a venture studio focused on building creator and media software. He is associated with Vid.AI, TubeMagic (formerly Morise.ai), moonn, and Warplabs. His work has been covered by outlets including The Quint and New York Weekly.

Early life and education

Born and raised in Deoghar, Jharkhand, Priyam studied computer science at BIT Mesra. He began building internet products early and later moved to Bangalore to focus on startups.

Career

  • Founded Morise.ai, an AI YouTube workflow tool later acquired and rebranded as TubeMagic.
  • Co-founded Vid.AI, a generative video platform for social-first teams and creators.
  • Built a portfolio of creator tools and platforms under the Warplabs and Priyam Ventures umbrella.
