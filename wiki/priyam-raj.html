Overview

Priyam Raj is a serial founder and operator based in Bangalore, India. He has built and exited two internet companies, and currently operates a venture studio focused on creator and media software. His work has been covered by outlets including The Quint and New York Weekly.

Early life

Priyam was born in Purnia, Bihar, India, where he lived until 2012. His family then moved to Deoghar, Jharkhand, where he completed his schooling at DAV Public School. In 2023, after graduating from BIT Mesra with a degree in Computer Science, he relocated to Bangalore to focus on building internet startups.

He began experimenting with internet products during college, starting with small projects and gradually building skills in product design, software development, and growth.

He's a frequent business class traveler, a fan of solo movie theater visits, and has expressed interest in eventually relocating to Europe, particularly the Swiss Alps.
