Overview

TubeMagic is an AI tool for YouTube creators that helps with research, scripting, and workflow automation. It evolved from Morise.ai, a solo-built product by Priyam Raj.

Origins

Morise.ai launched as a bootstrapped product and gained traction with creator communities. The tool was later acquired and rebranded as TubeMagic under new leadership.

Recognition

  • Featured on Product Hunt and earned “Product of the Day” recognition through GrowthSchool.
  • Reported adoption by creator teams and large content organizations.
