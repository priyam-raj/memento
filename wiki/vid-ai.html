Overview

Vid.AI is a generative video platform that helps teams produce short-form content quickly. The product targets creators, marketers, and social media teams who need high-volume output with consistent brand templates.

History

The project launched after the founders acquired the vid.ai domain in 2024. Early growth was driven by creator communities and social-first marketing. Press coverage in 2025 highlighted the product’s rapid traction.

Reported milestones

  • Reported to have crossed $250K in revenue within the first few months.
  • Referenced in coverage as a multi-million-dollar valued startup by early 2025.
  • Listed among top MRR products in creator tooling rankings.

Metrics are based on public statements and press coverage.
