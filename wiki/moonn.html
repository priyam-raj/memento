Overview

moonn is a platform designed for creators to run communities, sell courses, and manage subscriptions. The product aligns with the broader creator tooling ecosystem in Priyam’s venture portfolio.

Product direction

The platform focuses on simplifying payments and community management. It has been positioned as an alternative to modern course and creator platforms, with an emphasis on smooth onboarding and payout flows.

Brand styling is intentionally lowercase: “moonn”.
