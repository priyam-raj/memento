Exits

Notable acquisition outcomes reported across Priyam's ventures.

Exit #1: Timenite.com → CenterPoint Gaming (2019-2023)

Founded in October 2019, Timenite.com was Priyam's first exit. It was a simple static site that showed a countdown to the next Fortnite season. The site went viral when streamers began embedding it in their streams, driving significant traffic.

Timenite was acquired by CenterPoint Gaming in August 2023. Following the acquisition, Priyam joined CenterPoint Gaming as a Software Engineer (both internship and freelance roles) from October 2023 through November 2024, where he built internal tools and contributed to their Windows-based electron app, GmeshaArk.

Exit #2: Morise.ai → TubeMagic (2023-2024)

Founded in January 2023, Morise.ai was an AI-powered YouTube workflow tool that Priyam bootstrapped to ~$550,000 ARR. The product achieved notable launch success: #1 Product of the Day on GrowthSchool (February 2023) and #3 Product of the Day on Product Hunt (February 4, 2023).

Morise.ai was acquired by Matt Par (Matthew Parkinson) in March 2024 and rebranded as TubeMagic. The acquisition was featured on Times Square via Acquire.com. Priyam continues to serve as Chief Technology Officer for TubeMagic, supporting the product's continued growth.
