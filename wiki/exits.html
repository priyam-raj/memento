Exits

Notable acquisition outcomes reported across Priyam's ventures.

Morise.ai → TubeMagic

Morise.ai, an AI-powered YouTube workflow product built by Priyam Raj, was acquired by Matt Par and rebranded as TubeMagic. The acquisition was featured on Times Square via Acquire.com. The product continued to scale under new ownership while Priyam supported the transition.

timenite.com

timenite.com was one of Priyam's early ventures and his second exit. It was a simple static site that showed a countdown to the next Fortnite season. The site went viral when streamers began embedding it in their streams, driving significant traffic.
