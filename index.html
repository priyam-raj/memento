Founder instinct

I like useful products, clear distribution, and asking for things most people would talk themselves out of.

Like buying a domain for $47k, partnering with creators, or making the kind of public ask most people would talk themselves out of. Somebody has to move before the bet looks obvious.

I care most about products where the engineering, offer, and distribution all have to be right. That is usually where the real leverage is hiding.
